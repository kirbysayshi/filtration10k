Epidemic

press spacebar or click to start

Cells: green is clean, red is infected. Over 75 means the cell will deploy antibodies. Under 75 will cause the cell to spread the virus.

Double click to boost a cell by 75.

Click and drag to move cells around: position matters!

Cells have three attributes:

  • strength of the particles it ejects (the thickness of the charging ring)
  • resistance to other particles (the outer ring)
  • speed of particle ejection (the charging ring's arc)

Your goal is to clear all cells of infection!

Hold spacebar to see who cells will transmit to.

Win

You destroyed the infection! Next round in 5 seconds...
